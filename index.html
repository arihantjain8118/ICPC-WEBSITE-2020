About ICPC Asia West Gwalior-Pune Regionals

The International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) is the largest computer programming contest in the world. ABV-IIITM Gwalior and IIIT Pune jointly organizing Asia West Gwalior-Pune ICPC Regional contest. The ICPC is an activity that provides college students with an opportunity to demonstrate and sharpen their problem-solving and computing skills. The contest is considered to be the “Olympics of Computer Programming”. ICPC Gwalior-Pune Regionals organizers welcomes all the participants on behalf of our ICPC Global Sponsors which are: Huawei, JetBrains, AWS Educate and IBM Quantum.

Registration


Important Notes:

  • This year because of current covid-19 pandemic there will be only one online contest to finalize the team participation for world final 2020, which will be held on August 18, 2021.
  • Team registration is open between July 24, 2021 to August 11, 2021. Registration fees per team is Rs.450 (UPI/Bank Transfer). Initially we plan for 700 teams for the contest, if it filled early than we may stop further registration of the teams before August 11.
  • Do not forget to upload the registration details (along with scan copy of proof of payment) after completing the team registration at ICPC Global in following Google form
  • World final will be held at Mosco in First week of October, 2021.
  • A team can submit the programs in C, C++, python or in JAVA.
  • Organizer is yet to finalize the proctoring contest or not. Contest rule may be different from other regional site. Rules will be updated at least one week before the contest.
  • It is assumed that probable world final contestant has taken at least 1st dose of covid vaccine by 1st week of August and 2nd dose of covid vaccine by 5th September, 2021. If required Global ICPC organizers can help you to get 2nd dose after 28 days.

Team Formation Rules:

  • A team consists of at the most 3 contestants (No reserved contestants allowed this year) and a coach from the same Institution/University/ College.
  • Single individual login at ICPC Global & same team formation
    • Each individual should only have one operational account at the ICPC global site.
    • Each individual should be a part of exactly one team, which means team formation should be the same across all the regionals where the individual is going to take part.
    • This also means that the team details (team name, email id etc) should be the same.
    • A team should register for at most two regionals sites for online contest, and any discrepancy would lead to disqualification.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does team members have to be from same institution?

    Yes, Team members have to be from same institution.

  • When is the team accepted in the Baylor Site?

    On the same day of payment received it will be accepted in the Baylor site.

  • Time taken for the payment to get updated?

    It will take minimum three working days.

  • How many member should be in a team? Is it mandatory to keep a reserve person?

    There should be three members in a team, including reserve person four members. It is not mandatory to keep a reserve person.

  • What is the role of the Coach?
    • Coach is the faculty member from the same college as that of the team.
    • Coach creates a team in the Baylor Site under “Asia Amritapuri First Round Online Programming Contest” Online contest.
    • Coach is expected to create a team with the official email id. Official email id means, he/she shall use the institutional email id. While creating teams, the coach shall invite the contestants and reserve.
  • What is the role of reserved person? Will the reserved person get the certificate?

    In case of any emergency to the team in the absence of any person the reserved can participate. Yes, the reserved person will get the certificate.

  • What is the selection process for Onsite Contest?

    Based on the performance of the online round, each regional site will decide on the number of teams to be invited for the on-site round.

  • How to get the previous year certificate?

    You can login to the Baylor site icpc.baylor.edu and you can get the Certificate from the dashboard.

  • How can we know whether our payment is received?

    Once the payment is received your status will be updated in our Google sheet and also accepted in the Baylor site.

How to Reach?


How to reach ABV-IIITM Gwalior:

  • ABV-IIITM is located on Morena Link Road at a distance of about 3.5km and 8.5km from Gwalior railway station and Rajmata Vijay Raje Scindia city airport respectively.
  • The institute is easily accessible by road, and there are cabs and auto-rickshaws to commute from railway station or airport.

How to reach IIIT Pune:

  • IIIT Pune is at a distance of about 15km,8.4km and 21km from Pune railway station, Katraj Bus Stand and Pune Airport respectively.
  • The institute is easily accessible by road, and there are cabs and auto-rickshaws to commute from railway station, bus stand or airport.
